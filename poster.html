Everything Everywhere All At Once Poster

One of my hobbies is digital painting by opening a blank canvas in Photoshop and using the brush tool to paint. This is a digital painting that I created of the movie Everything Everywhere All At Once. I wanted to have a circle in the middle of the poster where the top half of the circle matched the colours of the googly eye in the movie and the bottom half of the circle matched the colours of the bagel in the movie. The googly eye and bagel are important metaphors in the film that represent the film's main themes, so I wanted them to be featured at the center of my painting but in a unique way. I used Evelyn's piano, Alpha Waymond's circular portals, Evelyn's googly eye, the rock's googly eye, and Evelyn's washing machine to form the top half of the circle representing the googly eye. I used Jobu's eye makeup, Raccacoonie's tail, Jobu's bagel, Deirdre's circle on the taxes paper, and Waymond's fanny pack to form the bottom half of the circle representing the bagel.



Laurier UX Design Program Poster

For a graphic design project, I was tasked with creating a poster to promote Wilfrid Laurier University’s User Experience Design program which is where I completed my bachelor's degree. The requirements for this poster were that it must include the university and program’s name, Laurier’s logo, Laurier’s colours (purple, gold, and red), a statement about the program, the link to Laurier's website, and all images and artwork besides the logo had to be original work created by me.

I started by thinking about what I wanted my statement to be. After coming up with my statement, I created images to tell a story related to my statement and images related to courses that the UX design program offered. I wanted to highlight the user-centered approach we use in the program and the different areas of design that we cover in my poster through the text and visuals. I used Adobe Illustrator to create this poster.
